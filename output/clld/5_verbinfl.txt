## Person prefixes {#sec:verbperson}
Verbs are inflected for person with a set of prefixes, shown in <a href='#tab:verbprefixes' class='crossref' name='tab:verbprefixes' >ref</a>.
First and second person prefixes show accusative alignment, expressing <span class="smallcaps">s</span> and <span class="smallcaps">p</span>.
Intransitive verbs are not overtly inflected for third person, while transitive verbs show an optional [Morpheme t3](MorphsetTable?#cldf:t3) in <span class="smallcaps">3</span>><span class="smallcaps">3</span> scenarios. <span title='Check: not in alternation with preceding O NP?'>❓</span>
An exception to this is the verb [Morpheme te-go](MorphsetTable?#cldf:te-go) 'to go', which shows an idiosyncratic prefix [Morph i32](MorphTable?#cldf:i32). <span title='"*ij‑të‑ri* ‘he goes’ plus 2 more" -- which ones?'>❓</span>

|:---------------------------------|:----------------------------------------|:----------------------------------------|
| <span class="smallcaps">1</span> | [Morpheme u1](MorphsetTable?#cldf:u1)   | [Morpheme u1](MorphsetTable?#cldf:u1)   |
| <span class="smallcaps">2</span> | [Morpheme me2](MorphsetTable?#cldf:me2) | [Morpheme me2](MorphsetTable?#cldf:me2) |
