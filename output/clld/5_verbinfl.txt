## Person prefixes {#sec:verbperson}
Verbs are inflected for person with a set of prefixes, shown in <a href='#tab:verbprefixes' class='crossref' name='tab:verbprefixes' release="False" >ref</a>.
First and second person prefixes show accusative alignment, expressing <span class='smallcaps'>s</span> and <span class='smallcaps'>p</span>.
Intransitive verbs are not overtly inflected for third person, while transitive verbs show an optional [Morpheme t3](MorphsetTable?no_translation#cldf:t3) in <span class='smallcaps'>3</span>><span class='smallcaps'>3</span> scenarios. 
An exception to this is the verb [Morpheme te-go](MorphsetTable?#cldf:te-go) 'to go', which shows an idiosyncratic prefix [Morph i32](MorphTable?#cldf:i32).

|                                  | <span class='smallcaps'>intr</span>                   | <span class='smallcaps'>tr</span>                     |
|:---------------------------------|:------------------------------------------------------|:------------------------------------------------------|
| <span class='smallcaps'>1</span> | [Morpheme u1](MorphsetTable?no_translation#cldf:u1)   | [Morpheme u1](MorphsetTable?no_translation#cldf:u1)   |
| <span class='smallcaps'>2</span> | [Morpheme me2](MorphsetTable?no_translation#cldf:me2) | [Morpheme me2](MorphsetTable?no_translation#cldf:me2) |
| <span class='smallcaps'>3</span> | ∅                                                     | [Morpheme t3](MorphsetTable?no_translation#cldf:t3)   |

* [Morpheme t3](MorphsetTable?translation=3>3#cldf:t3) attested on one V in the pan‑Cariban “progressive” construction w/ 2nd person <span class='smallcaps'>a</span> 
* Ø‑ ‘<span class='smallcaps'>3p</span>’ with transitive verbs with <span class='smallcaps'>1a</span> or <span class='smallcaps'>2a</span>; also sometimes <span class='smallcaps'>3a</span>
* one example of ([Morpheme me2](MorphsetTable?#cldf:me2)) ‘<span class='smallcaps'>2A</span>’ on imperative verb 
* Note that all transitive verbs are consonant‑initial, whether etymologically or not because [Morpheme ylk](MorphsetTable?#cldf:ylk) is added to all vowel‑initial roots
    * the _y‑_ disappears when preceded by the detransitivizer
