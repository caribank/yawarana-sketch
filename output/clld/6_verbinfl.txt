## Person prefixes {#sec:verbperson}
Verbs are inflected for person with a set of prefixes, shown in <a href='#tab:verbprefixes' class='crossref' name='tab:verbprefixes' >ref</a>.
First and second person prefixes show ergative alignment, expressing <span class='smallcaps'>s</span> and <span class='smallcaps'>p</span>. <span title='count cases of expressing A'>❗️</span>
Intransitive verbs are not overtly inflected for third person, while transitive verbs show an optional [Morpheme ta-3](morphemes.csv?no_translation#cldf:ta-3) in <span class='smallcaps'>3</span>><span class='smallcaps'>3</span> scenarios.<span title='mentionworthy that this is not in alternation with preceding NPs'>❗️</span>
