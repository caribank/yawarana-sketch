## Transitive and intransitive roots and stems {#sec:verbroots}
[Language yab](LanguageTable?#cldf:yab) verb roots can be divided into those yielding an intransitive stem, and those yielding a transitive stem.
The only inflectional criterion distinguishing the two classes is the third person prefix [Morpheme t3](MorphsetTable?no_translation#cldf:t3), which can only occur on transitive stems.
Thus, transitive [Morpheme yawanka-kill](MorphsetTable?#cldf:yawanka-kill) can take [Morpheme t3](MorphsetTable?no_translation#cldf:t3) <a class="exref" example_id="convfemgrme-217"></a>, but intransitive [Morpheme yaruwa-laugh](MorphsetTable?#cldf:yaruwa-laugh) does not <a class="exref" example_id="convrisamaj-42"></a>.
