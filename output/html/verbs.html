The pluractional marker -pëtï

This aspect-marking morpheme is known from other Cariban languages psrc. This example illustrates its -pëj allomorph:

(ctorat-40) Yawarana tipa-pëj-se-jne waijta-jne
go.group-PLUR-PST-PL mouse-PL
‘las ratas se fueron’

-jpë

-jpë was originally a nominalizer, but now also functions as a simple past marker:

(anfoperso-02) Yawarana ana këyeta-jpë intipijkë ana chi-∅ yawë
1+3 grow.up-PST a.little 1+3 COP-IPFV LOC
‘nos criamos cuando estábamos chiquiticos nosotros’

It also occurs on nouns:

(anfoperso-17) Yawarana tawara ma-∅ ana këyeta-jpë ana papa pan pata-jpë të-∅
too throw-IPFV 1+3 grow.up-PST 1+3 father:VOC late axe-PST go-IPFV
‘así nos criamos después de que se murió mi papá en su pueblo’
