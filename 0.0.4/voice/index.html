    18. Detransitive voice

    • functions of detrz:
      • antipassive
      • passive
      • reflexive
      • reciprocal
      • anticausative
    • other strategies for removing participant:
      • -se-mï 'gnomic'
      • -në
    • what is not used for voice?
      • -sapë
      • participle

    18.1 Issues with transitivity

    • ya occurs with intransitives
    • some transitive verbs occur with oblique-marked P arguments:
    1. ati rë warai mërë iniri, irëjpë mëyarika ti
      ati ati what
      rë rë emp
      warai warai like
      mërë mërë 2pro
      iniri ini -ri see-ipfv
      irëjpë irëjpë then
      më =yarika më =yarika -∅ 2=laugh-ipfv
      ti ti hsy
      ‘You see something and then you laugh.’ (Conversación sobre la risa entre GrMe y ElPe : 1)
    • some intransitive verbs can look like transitives:
    1. ¿ati ejnë yaruwari?
      ati ati what
      ejnë ejnë 1+2pro
      yaruwari yaruwa -ri laugh-ipfv
      ‘What are we going to laugh about?’ (Conversación sobre la risa entre GrMe y ElPe : 12)
    • not all transitive verbs take identifiable objects:
    1. wïrë inija ta rë
      wïrë wïrë 1pro
      inija ini -ja see-neg
      ta ta like
      rë rë emp
      ‘I haven’t seen.’ (Conversación sobre la risa entre GrMe y ElPe : 44)
