    6. Verbal inflection

    ❕

    6.1 Person prefixes

    Verbs are inflected for person with a set of prefixes, shown in Table 6.1. ❔ First and second person prefixes show ergative alignment, expressing s and p. Intransitive verbs are not overtly inflected for third person, while transitive verbs show an optional ta- in 3>3 scenarios.❕

    Person marking prefixes on verbs
    intr tr
    1 u- u-
    2 më- më-
    1+2 ej- ej-
    3 ∅ ta-

    6.1.1 Examples

    • Second person A
      • më- marking A in
      • subordinate clauses and
      • is 'learn' transitive?
      1. michi ma mëyapëjjrama
        michi michi med.anim
        ma ma rst
        mëyapëjjrama më- yapëj -jrama 2-grab-proh
        ‘do not touch this (auto)’ (Cuento de Ochi y otros por MaFl : 324)
      2. mëinija ka, aniki pinchi, tënësem warai yichapë, okonotojpe
        mëinija më- ini -ja 2-see-neg
        ka ka q
        aniki aniki who
        pinchi pinchi hes
        tënësem   ***
        warai warai like
        yichapë   ***
        okonotojpe   ***
        ‘you have not seen a fish that is put on to dry (auto)’ (Historia de vida de una mujer y costumbres de los yabaranas : 633)
      3. tëwï ma takï mëyakarama chijpë wararë kwa ta sënkatoj mëtë
        tëwï tëwï 3pro
        ma ma rst
        takï takï cnfrm
        mëyakarama më- -∅ 2-tell-ipfv
        chijpë chi -jpë cop-pst.acnnmlz
        wara =rë wara =rë thus=emp
        kwa kwa how
        ta ta like
        sënkatoj -toj finish-circ.nmlz
        mëtë   ?
        ‘tell us again how they ended up there (auto)’ (Conversation about family with María Flores and Pablo Pérez : 13)
      1. mërë warë mëyënëtojpano ka uyakërë mërë wepïrï
        mërë mërë 2pro
        wa =rë wa =rë thus=emp
        mëyënëtojpano më- yënë -tojpano 2-eat(meat)-fut.concl
        ka   ?
        uyakërë u- y- akërë 1-lk-with
        mërë mërë 2pro
        wepïrï wepï -rï come-ipfv
        ‘so for you to eat you came with me? (auto)’ (Conversación sobre malos augurios y otros : 43)
      2. mëyakarama mare
        mëyakarama më- -∅ 2-tell-ipfv
        mare mare rel.inan
        ‘the one you are saying (auto)’ (Cuento de la araña : 5)
      1. kwase mëëmpamïjpë ejnë waimu yaye
        kwase kwase how
        mëëmpamïjpë më- ëmpamï -jpë 2-learn-pst
        ejnë ejnë 1+2pro
        waimu waimu -∅ language-pert
        yaye yaye loc
        ‘how you learned our language (auto)’ (Conversation about family with María Flores and Pablo Pérez : 15)
      2. irëjpë, kwaraijyawë rë mëëmpamïjpë tajto marë ti?
        irëjpë irëjpë then
        kwaraijyawë   ***
        rë rë emp
        mëëmpamïjpë më- ëmpamï -jpë 2-learn-pst
        tajto   ***
        marë marë rel.inan
        ti ti hsy
        ‘Afterwards, when did you learn what you say? (auto)’ (Conversation about family with María Flores and Pablo Pérez : 238)

    1. uyepematojpe pïrarë wïrë inawë
      uyepematojpe u- yepema -tojpe 1-pay-purp

      inawë inawë have
      ‘I can't afford to pay (auto)’ (Historia de vida de una mujer y costumbres de los yabaranas : 875)
    • Transitive local scenarios
      1. entë mëinpojra wïrë ya
        entë entë here.loc
        mëinpo =jra më- in -po -∅ =jra 2-see-des-ipfv=neg
        wïrë wïrë 1pro
        ya ya erg
        ‘I don't want to see them here (auto)’ (Conversación sobre malos augurios y otros : 231)
      2. mëini wïrë ya
        mëini më- ini -∅ 2-see-ipfv
        wïrë wïrë 1pro
        ya ya erg
        ‘te voy a ver (auto)’ (Conversación sobre malos augurios y otros : 232)
      3. tëwï ke ma mëyepema wïrë ya, ta ti ta
        tëwï tëwï 3pro
        ke ke ins
        ma ma rst
        mëyepema më- yepema -∅ 2-pay-ipfv
        wïrë wïrë 1pro
        ya ya erg
        ta ta -∅ say-ipfv
        ti ti hsy
        ta ta like
        ‘I'll pay you with this," he said (auto)’ (Cuento de Ochi y otros por MaFl : 283)
    • one attested case of ta- on the lexical verb of a -pëkë construction w/ 2nd person a on aux ❕
      • Ø‑ ‘3p’ with transitive verbs with 1a or 2a
    • one example of më- ‘2a’ on imperative verb ❕
    • *t‑V‑se is no more — the t‑ is gone, except in lexicalized items

    6.2 Main clause tense‑aspect‑mood‑polarity suffixes

    Verbs in main clauses are inflected for TAMP with a set of suffixes, shown in Table 6.2. They are discussed in 6.2.1.

    Verbal TAM suffixes
    Suffix Function
    -ri imperfective
    -jpë past
    -se past perfective
    -sapë perfect
    -sarë imminent future
    -nëpëkë prog.intr
    pëkë prog.tr
    ‑tojpano fut
    (-tojpe) fut
    -ja neg
    -se-mï ‘obligation’
    -në impersonal S
    -topo
    Non-declarative suffixes
    Suffix Function
    ‑jrama proh
    -i juss
    -kë / ‑të-kë imp / imp.pl
    -ta / -tantë-kë imp.mot / imp.mot.pl

    Misc:

    • -se=pano ‘pst=concl’
    • -saj=pano ‘pfv=concl’
    • -sarë=pano ‘imn=concl’

    6.2.1 -ri

    • allomorphy:
      • most conservative form: -rï
      • /ï/ > /i/: -ri
      • phonetic loss: -∅
        • inconsistent zero before jra, see 9
        • consistent zero before kontomo
      • assimilation: -ru
    • gave rise to -ri
    • plural with kontomo
    • negated with jra , but potentially two distinct etymologies
    • emerging pattern: -ri with postverbal (possessed?) arguments?
    1. wïrë yaruwarijra
      wïrë wïrë 1pro
      yaruwari =jra yaruwa -ri =jra laugh-ipfv=neg
      ‘I don’t laugh.’ (Conversación sobre la risa entre GrMe y ElPe : 4)

    6.2.1.1 Semantics

    • not specified for tense, just imperfective aspect:
      • past
      • future
      • gnomic/present?
    1. irëjpë tëwï waijtatomo nwajtëri
      irëjpë irëjpë then
      tëwï tëwï 3pro
      waijtatomo waijta -tomo mouse-pl
      nwajtëri nwajtë -ri dance-ipfv
      ‘Then the mice were dancing.’ (Cuento de las ratas por AnFo : 16)
    1. ¿ kwase ejnë yaruwari?
      kwase kwase how
      ejnë ejnë 1+2pro
      yaruwari yaruwa -ri laugh-ipfv
      ‘How will we laugh?’ (Conversación sobre la risa entre GrMe y ElPe : 6)
      1. wïrë yaruwarijra
        wïrë wïrë 1pro
        yaruwari =jra yaruwa -ri =jra laugh-ipfv=neg
        ‘I don’t laugh.’ (Conversación sobre la risa entre GrMe y ElPe : 4)
      2. uyïwïj yawë usenejkari sukuri jwama
        uyïwïj u- y- ïwïj -∅ 1-lk-house-pert
        yawë yawë loc
        usenejkari u- -ri 1-stay-ipfv
        sukuri sukuri silently
        jwama   ***
        ‘I silently stay in my house.’ (Conversación sobre la risa entre GrMe y ElPe : 28)

    6.2.2 -jpë

    • allomorphy: none? ❕
    • diachrony: from -jpë
    • negated with jra
    1. tëwïsantomo wïrë wejyatë këyetajpëjra
      tëwïsantomo tëwïsantomo 3pro.pl
      wïrë wïrë 1pro
      wejyatë   ***
      këyetajpë =jra -jpë =jra grow-pst=neg
      ‘they did not grow up with me (auto)’ (Historia de vida de una mujer y costumbres de los yabaranas : 186)

    6.2.3 -se

    • allomorphy: -se/-che
    • diachrony: from -se
    • negation: replaced with -ja (see also 9.1.1)
    1. wëjkaja, ana tëse neke ne
      wëjkaja wëjka -ja fall-neg
      ana ana 1+3pro
      tëse të -se go-pst
      neke neke cntr
      ne ne ints
      ‘no nos caimos, nosotros nos fuimos (auto)’ (Conversación sobre los primeros tiempos de contacto : 28)
    • plural: kontomo
    1. tipasekontomo warë irëjpë pïrarë
      tipase =kontomo tipa -se =kontomo go_in_group-pst=pl
      wa =rë wa =rë thus=emp
      irëjpë irëjpë then
      pïrarë pïrarë neg.exist
      ‘They left and were gone.’ (Cuento de las ratas por AnFo : 30)

    6.2.4 -sapë

    • diachrony: from -sapë
    • distribution: only occurs on the copula?
    • allomorphy: -sapë and -saj
    • negation: with -ja on lexical verb ❔
    1. irë wejtane mujyampe patakaja wejsapë
      irë irë 3ana.inan
      wejtane wej -tane cop-cncs
      mujyam =pe mujyam =pe pregnancy=ess
      patakaja -ja take_out-neg
      wejsapë wej -sapë cop-pfv
      ‘despite this, she did not get pregnant (auto)’ (Cuento de la araña : 38)
    1. apatakaja pïnïka wejsapë
      apatakaja -ja come_out-neg
      pïnïka   ***
      wejsapë wej -sapë cop-pfv
      ‘maybe she did not come out (pregnant) (auto)’ (Cuento de la araña : 39)
    1. tayakïjtëja pïnika wejsapë
      tayakïjtëja   ***
      pïnika pïnika prob
      wejsapë wej -sapë cop-pfv
      ‘maybe he didn't sleep with her (auto)’ (Cuento de la araña : 40)

    1. pïrarë ti iwenaru wejsapë
      pïrarë pïrarë neg.exist
      ti ti hsy
      iwenaru i- wena -ru 3-vomit-pert
      wejsapë wej -sapë cop-pfv
      ‘their vomit was not there.’ (Cuento de las ratas por AnFo : 19)

    6.2.5 -sarë

    • once a converb, now 'imminent future'
    1. irëjpë ta ti ta konopo wejsarë konopo wejsarë
      irëjpë irëjpë then
      ta ta -∅ say-ipfv
      ti ti hsy
      ta ta like
      konopo konopo rain
      wejsarë wej -sarë come-imn
      konopo konopo rain
      wejsarë wej -sarë come-imn
      ‘Then they said: “it’s raining, it’s raining”.’ (Cuento de las ratas por AnFo : 25)
    1. moyochi tasarë, moyochi chipokono kojpaye pïnika warotari
      moyochi moyochi spider(sp.)
      tasarë ta -sarë say-imn
      moyochi moyochi spider(sp.)
      chipokono chipokono because
      kojpaye at_night
      pïnika pïnika prob
      warotari warota -ri work-ipfv
      ‘called a spider, perhaps because the spider works at night. (auto)’ (Cuento de la araña : 25)
